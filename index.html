Dear Colleagues!
On behalf of the Organisation Committee, we would like to extend invitation to join us at the Riga EAST CTO meeting in Riga from April 10th to 11th, 2025. The meeting is dedicated to interventional cardiologists with special interest to CTO PCIs and complex coronary interventions. Meeting traditionaly focuses on technical and practical aspects of CTO PCI. Experienced CTO operators during 2 days will perform 10 complex CTO cases. Parallel with the live case, there will be an opportunity to participate in discussions, listen to interesting presentations, visit an industry exhibition, and participate in hands-on sessions. We look forward to welcoming you in Riga for two very interesting and interactive days! We encourage you to explore the program and start planning your participation. Warm regards,
Dr. Artis Kalnins
